Dr Christopher Carr

About

I am a mathematician and cryptographer specialising in blockchain technology. My research focuses on security, scalability, decentralisation, and energy use in distributed systems.

My teaching has included Blockchain, Cryptography, Number Theory, Information Security, and Fintech. I designed and delivered a Smart Contracts lecture series that ran in 2023 and continued in 2024 and 2025.

Before Exeter, I held positions at UWE Bristol and NTNU, and also worked as a subject matter expert in cryptography and blockchain for Deloitte Oslo.

I completed a joint PhD between NTNU and QUT in 2019. Thesis title: Towards Fairness and Decentralisation in Modern Cryptocurrencies.

Alongside academic work, I am connected to blockchain startup work including Graphchain and NO/WALLET, and serve as CTO at Siignd.
